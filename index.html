Subhankar Ghosh

I am a graduate student in the Computer Science Department at Washington State University, Pullman, where I am advised by Yan Yan and Jana Doppa . I work on computer vision and theoretical machine learning. Before joining here, I did my masters in Aerospace Engineering department from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I did my undergrad in Civil Engineering Department from Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, Kolkata.

I am interested to do research in the uncertainty quantification domain. Modern machine learning approaches can not quantify any certainty about their predictions, I try to develop new algorithm and theory to give certainty about the black-bos predictions.
